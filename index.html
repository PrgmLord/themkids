Special classes for children
from 1 year to 7

Group of Early Development
"Classes with mom"

1 — 2 years old

  • Classes allow to develop cognitive, physical and social skills in a playful way

  • Develop memory, thinking and sensory abilities (color, shape, size, tactile sensation).

Sport helps the child develop cognitive, physical and social skills in a comfortable position for each child, in an accessible form is for it, and most importantly - in the presence of a loved one (mom, dad or grandmother).

Groups of early maturity

2 — 5 years old

  • Learn to develop their thoughts, to correlate, analyze, creative approach to problem solving.

  • Faced with the task of self-control and self-organization.

  • Interact with adults and peers, learn how to protect themselves and respect others.

Each class has its own unique theme, and constantly shifting activity does not get bored and give overdo child. In the classroom creates an welcoming and accepting atmosphere that allows the child to open up and do better each lesson for themselves the most effective.

Preparing for school

5 — 7 years old

  • We teach reading, writing, mathematics and logic.

  • Psychological and organizational adaptation to school.

  • The kid knows what the team, how to deal with classmates who have mastered the rules of etiquette.

In our time, the future first-grader should have a solid knowledge base to go to school. Of course, a child can and should do at home. But to great effect, we recommend you to visit a special class created to help you and your baby.
